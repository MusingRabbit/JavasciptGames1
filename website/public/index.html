Temple

The temple scene was the first scene I had a crack at making on my own, outside of the hand-holding of the UWS.

The lighting or the textures never really came out the way I liked them to, so I enabled a glow layer to bring about this bloom effect where all the emmissive colours really pop. It gave the scene a somewhat ethereal look.

Physics

A simple physics simulation to simulate gravity. There are 2000 boxes within the scene and one sphere acting in the role of a planet or a star.

The boxes will be attracted to the sphere, until it is clicked on by which point the "star" will go black and all of the boxes will be repelled.

There is supposed to be a boundary in this scene to prevent the boxes from floating too far away, but it doesn't work. So in the event that that happens - click the 'Reset' button to reset the scene.
